Quick Safety Tips

Recognizing Bullying
  • Repeated aggressive behavior
  • Power imbalances
  • Intentional harm (physical, verbal, social)
  • Targeting of differences
What You Can Do
  • Document incidents
  • Tell someone you trust
  • Use available reporting systems
  • Seek professional support
